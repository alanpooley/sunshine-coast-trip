Our 8-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to Caloundra

Spend the morning at Kings Beach, enjoy lunch at Moffat Beach Brewing Co., and explore the coastal walk. Wrap up the day with dinner at Bocca Italian.

Day 2: Montville & Scenic Hinterland

Visit Montville Village in the morning, explore Gardners Falls, and enjoy dinner at Spirit House Restaurant.

Day 3: Coastal Relaxation and Exploration

Relax at Dicky Beach, have lunch at Greedy Gringo, and visit Buderim Forest Park in the afternoon. End the day with dinner at Warrior Restaurant & Bar.

Day 4: Scenic Sunshine Coast

Start with breakfast at Groundskeeper Willie Coffee Roasters, visit Point Cartwright, and enjoy lunch at Alex Surf Club. Spend the afternoon at Kawana Beach and have dinner at Matso's Sunshine Coast Brewery.

Day 5: Noosa Adventure

Travel to Noosa, have lunch at Knock Knock Noosa, explore Noosa National Park, and enjoy dinner at The Doonan.

Day 6: Nature and Leisure

Visit Kondalilla Falls, explore Mapleton Falls Lookout, and have lunch at The Surf Club Mooloolaba. Spend the afternoon at Eumundi Markets or Mooloolaba Beach, and end with dinner at Your Mates Brewing Co.

Day 7: Local Gems and Relaxation

Spend the morning at Shelly Beach, hike up Emu Mountain, and enjoy lunch at Nurcha Kawana. Explore Maroochy Regional Bushland Botanic Garden, and have dinner at Terella Brewing.

Day 8: Farewell to the Sunshine Coast

Relax at Moffat Beach, visit the Caloundra Street Fair, and conclude your trip with a visit to the Glass House Mountains Lookout.
